Loading Message (Easy)



With dnSpy:


Open up your APK's "Assembly-CSharp.dll" (found in [APK]/assets/bin/Data/Managed/)
Search "LoadingViewModule"
Scroll down until you find two loading screen messages
The one labelled CN is the Chinese loading message and the EN is, of course, English
Edit it to whatever you want it to be [EX: "Loading..." to "Hello World"]
Save module and if you've done it correctly, your epic new loading screen text will appear how you wrote it in
